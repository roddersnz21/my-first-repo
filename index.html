About Us

Making neapolitan style pizza for over 20 years

Hero Pizza is a family owned pizzeria crazy about pizza. Our focus is to offer the best Neapolitan style pizza in Dublin. Our pizzas are freshly made and baked in a woodfire oven. We aim to bring excellent quality, unforgettable flavours and great service. That's how we conquered our loyal customers and became one of the top pizzerias in Ireland.

Fresh Ingredients

In pursuit of the best Italian gastronomic excellence we create new pizzas and continuously update the menu with our organically grown flour, vegetables and herbs.
